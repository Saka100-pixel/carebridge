How it works

From request to bedside — simply.

01

Post your care request

Describe the patient's condition, type of care needed, your location in Lusaka, and preferred shift times.

02

Browse verified matches

See nearby licensed professionals matched to your needs — each with their NMCZ/HPCZ badge, ratings, experience, and hourly rate.

03

Book a shift

Choose an 8-hour, 12-hour, or 24-hour shift. Pay securely via MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, or card.

04

Care delivered at home

Your professional arrives. Payment is released only after the shift is completed. Both sides leave a review.
